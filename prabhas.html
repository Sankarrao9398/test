PRABHAS (Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju)

Prabhas is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Telugu cinema and is one of the most popular and highest-paid actors in India. Born on 23 October 1979, Prabhas gained nationwide fame with S.S. Rajamouli’s epic series Baahubali. He is known for his dedication, charm, and versatile performances, making him a pan-India star loved by millions.

BAAHUBALI: THE BEGINNING & THE CONCLUSION

Baahubali (2015, 2017) directed by S.S. Rajamouli made Prabhas an international star. He played the dual role of Amarendra Baahubali and Mahendra Baahubali, showcasing strength, charisma, and royal elegance. The movies became global blockbusters and made him a household name.

SAAHO (2019)

Saaho was a high-octane action thriller starring Prabhas alongside Shraddha Kapoor. Released in multiple languages, it showcased Prabhas as an undercover cop in a stylish action-packed avatar with breathtaking stunts.

RADHE SHYAM (2022)

Radhe Shyam was a romantic drama where Prabhas played a world-famous palmist, Vikramaditya. Paired with Pooja Hegde, the film explored destiny, love, and fate, shot across Europe with stunning visuals.

Kalki 2898 AD (2024)

Kalki 2898 AD is a futuristic sci-fi epic directed by Nag Ashwin. Prabhas plays Bhairava, a rebel warrior in a dystopian world set thousands of years in the future. The film blends Indian mythology with science fiction, co-starring Amitabh Bachchan, Kamal Haasan, and Deepika Padukone.

SALAAR (2023)

Directed by Prashanth Neel, Salaar is a pan-India action drama where Prabhas plays Deva, a powerful character in an intense story of friendship, loyalty, and war. The film further established him as an action superstar.
